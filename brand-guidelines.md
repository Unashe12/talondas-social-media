# Talondas Ltd - Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Company Name:** Talondas Ltd  
**Tagline:** "Your Partner in Marketing Success"  
**Industry:** Marketing & Creative Agency

## 🎨 Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue:** `#0056B3` - Trust, professionalism, stability
- **Accent Orange:** `#FF6B35` - Energy, creativity, innovation

### Secondary Colors
- **Dark Gray:** `#2C3E50` - Sophistication, text
- **Light Gray:** `#ECF0F1` - Backgrounds, subtle elements
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` - Clean space, clarity

### Color Usage
- **Headers & CTAs:** Primary Blue or Accent Orange
- **Body Text:** Dark Gray
- **Backgrounds:** White or Light Gray
- **Accents:** Orange for highlights and important elements

## 🔤 Typography

### Font Family
- **Headings:** Montserrat Bold (700) - Modern, professional
- **Subheadings:** Montserrat SemiBold (600)
- **Body Text:** Open Sans Regular (400) - Readable, clean
- **Accents:** Montserrat Bold (700)

### Font Sizes
- **Main Headline:** 48px
- **Subheading:** 32px
- **Body:** 16px
- **Small Text:** 12px

## 📐 Logo Guidelines

### Minimum Size
- **Digital:** 200px width
- **Print:** 2 inches width

### Clear Space
- Maintain 20px padding around logo on all sides
- Never crop or distort logo

### Logo Variations
1. Full Logo with Text
2. Logo with Icon Only
3. Horizontal Layout
4. Vertical Layout
5. Monochrome Version

## 📝 Tone & Voice

- **Professional yet Approachable**
- **Clear and Concise**
- **Innovative and Forward-thinking**
- **Client-centric and Results-driven**

## ✅ Design Principles

1. **Simplicity** - Clean, uncluttered designs
2. **Consistency** - Unified brand experience
3. **Relevance** - Content aligned with audience
4. **Hierarchy** - Clear visual structure
5. **Accessibility** - Readable, inclusive design
