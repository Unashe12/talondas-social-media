## 📱 Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram
- Use 1080x1080 px for feed posts
- Create 1080x1920 px stories
- Include 15-30 hashtags
- Post at 6-9 AM, 12 PM, or 7-11 PM EST

### LinkedIn
- Use 1200x627 px dimensions
- Focus on professional insights
- 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Post Tuesday-Thursday, 8 AM-2 PM EST

### Facebook
- 1200x628 px recommended
- Can post 1-2x daily
- Include video for higher engagement
- Best times: Wednesday-Sunday, 1-3 PM EST

### Twitter/X
- 1024x512 px or square 1080x1080 px
- Keep text concise (280 characters)
- Use 1-2 trending hashtags
- Post 3-5x daily, Mon-Thu 9-10 AM or 5-6 PM EST

### TikTok
- Vertical 1080x1920 px format
- 15-60 second videos
- Use trending sounds & effects
- Post daily for best algorithm performance

## 🎯 Success Metrics to Track

Monitor these for each post:

- **Reach:** Total people who saw your post
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
- **Click-Through Rate:** How many clicked your links
- **Follower Growth:** New followers per post
- **Conversion Rate:** Posts that led to desired action

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Consistency is Key**
   - Post at regular intervals
   - Maintain visual consistency
   - Use brand colors in every post

2. **Mobile-First Design**
   - Always preview on mobile
   - Use large, readable fonts
   - Leave sufficient padding

3. **Engagement = Success**
   - Reply to comments within 1 hour
   - Ask questions to encourage replies
   - Tag relevant accounts

4. **Test & Learn**
   - Try different posting times
   - A/B test different designs
   - Analyze what works best

5. **Keep It Fresh**
   - Rotate between content types
   - Use user-generated content
   - Celebrate team & client wins

## 🆘 Need Help?

### Common Questions:

**Q: How do I change the colors?**
A: Look for hex color codes like `#0056B3` and replace with your own. Use `brand-guidelines.md` as reference.

**Q: Can I use different fonts?**
A: Yes! But stick to Montserrat and Open Sans for brand consistency.

**Q: What size should my images be?**
A: Check `platform-specs.md` for exact dimensions for each platform.

**Q: How do I export as an image?**
A: 
- HTML/CSS: Take screenshot or use tools like Headless Chrome
- SVG: Open in browser, right-click → Save as image
- Figma: Click Export button
- Adobe XD: File → Export

**Q: When should I post?**
A: See `content-calendar.md` for optimal times by platform.
